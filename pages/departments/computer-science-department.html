Department of Computer Science

Department of Computer Science

The Department of Computer Science at Nalanda University is at the forefront of computing education and research, from artificial intelligence to cybersecurity. Our cutting-edge labs and distinguished faculty provide students with an exceptional environment for learning and innovation in computing technologies.

Our Mission

To advance the frontiers of computing through innovative research and to educate the next generation of computer scientists who will drive technological advancement and digital transformation across industries.

Programs Offered

Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science

A four-year undergraduate program providing a strong foundation in computer science fundamentals, with opportunities for specialization in various domains of computing.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Mathematics and Physics
  • Intake: 60 students per year
View Syllabus

Master of Technology in Computer Science

An advanced two-year program offering specialized training in cutting-edge computing technologies, preparing students for industry leadership roles or doctoral studies.

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Eligibility: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
  • Intake: 30 students per year
View Syllabus

Ph.D. in Computer Science

A research-focused doctoral program for students pursuing original contributions to computer science, with opportunities to work alongside leading researchers in the field.

  • Duration: 3-5 years
  • Eligibility: Master's degree in Computer Science with research experience
  • Intake: Based on research openings

Laboratories and Facilities

Our department is equipped with modern laboratories and facilities to support teaching and cutting-edge research:

Software Development Laboratory

Equipped with high-performance workstations and the latest software development tools for building robust applications across various platforms.

Artificial Intelligence Laboratory

Features specialized hardware for machine learning, deep learning frameworks, and AI development environments for research in intelligent systems.

Cybersecurity Laboratory

Includes network security testing equipment, penetration testing tools, and isolated environments for studying security vulnerabilities and defenses.

Data Science Laboratory

A dedicated space with specialized software and computing resources for big data analytics, data visualization, and statistical modeling.

Virtual Reality & Graphics Laboratory

Provides VR/AR equipment, motion capture systems, and powerful graphics workstations for immersive computing research and development.
