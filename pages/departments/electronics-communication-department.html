Department of Electronics and Communication

Department of Electronics and Communication

The Department of Electronics and Communication at Nalanda University is at the forefront of innovation in electronic systems, signal processing, and communication technologies. Our cutting-edge laboratories and experienced faculty provide students with an exceptional environment for learning and research in this rapidly evolving field.

Our Mission

To advance the frontiers of electronics and communication technologies through innovative research and to educate the next generation of engineers who will drive technological advancement and digital transformation across industries.

Programs Offered

Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Communication

A four-year undergraduate program providing a strong foundation in electronic systems, communication networks, and signal processing, with opportunities for specialization in various domains.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
  • Intake: 60 students per year
View Syllabus

Master of Technology in Electronics and Communication

An advanced two-year program offering specialized training in cutting-edge electronics and communication technologies, preparing students for industry leadership roles or doctoral studies.

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Eligibility: Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication or related field
  • Intake: 30 students per year
View Syllabus

Ph.D. in Electronics and Communication

A research-focused doctoral program for students pursuing original contributions to electronics and communication engineering, with opportunities to work alongside leading researchers in the field.

  • Duration: 3-5 years
  • Eligibility: Master's degree in Electronics and Communication with research experience
  • Intake: Based on research openings

Laboratories and Facilities

Our department is equipped with modern laboratories and facilities to support teaching and cutting-edge research:

VLSI and Embedded Systems Laboratory

Equipped with advanced FPGA development boards, ASIC design tools, and embedded system platforms for designing and testing integrated circuits and embedded applications.

Wireless Communication Laboratory

Features software-defined radios, spectrum analyzers, and network simulation tools for research in wireless protocols, 5G technologies, and IoT communication systems.

Signal Processing and Control Systems Laboratory

Includes DSP development platforms, real-time control systems, and signal analysis equipment for developing advanced signal processing algorithms and control applications.

RF and Microwave Engineering Laboratory

A specialized facility with vector network analyzers, microwave test benches, and antenna design tools for research in RF circuits, microwave systems, and electromagnetic applications.

IoT and Sensor Networks Laboratory

Provides development platforms for Internet of Things applications, sensor integration tools, and network testing equipment for building connected systems and smart devices.
