Faculty of Law

The Faculty of Law at Nalanda University is committed to excellence in legal education, research, and practice. We prepare our students to become competent legal professionals with a strong foundation in both theoretical and practical aspects of law.

Our Vision

To be a premier institution for legal education, fostering legal scholarship, promoting justice, and developing ethical legal professionals who contribute to society through law and policy.
