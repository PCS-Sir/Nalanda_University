Faculty of Arts

The Faculty of Arts at Nalanda University is a vibrant community of scholars and students dedicated to exploring human culture, creativity, and society. Our programs foster critical thinking, cultural understanding, and creative expression across diverse disciplines.

Our Vision

To be a leading center for humanities and social sciences education, promoting cultural understanding, critical inquiry, and creative expression while preserving and advancing knowledge of human civilization.
