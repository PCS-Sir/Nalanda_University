Faculty of Arts

Faculty of Arts

The Faculty of Arts at Nalanda University is dedicated to exploring human creativity, culture, and expression. Our programs foster critical thinking, creative expression, and a deep understanding of the humanities and social sciences, preparing students to engage meaningfully with diverse cultural contexts.

Our Vision

To cultivate creative thinkers, cultural leaders, and engaged citizens who contribute to society through artistic expression, cultural understanding, and humanistic inquiry.
