About Us

We are the world's largest university

Nalanda University stands as a modern revival of the ancient seat of learning, embodying a rich tradition of academic excellence that dates back to the 5th century. Our institution combines historical wisdom with contemporary educational approaches, offering diverse programs across multiple disciplines. With state-of-the-art facilities and a distinguished faculty, we provide an environment where students from around the world can engage in meaningful research and collaborative learning.
