Faculty of Engineering

The Faculty of Engineering at Nalanda University is dedicated to developing innovative engineers who can solve complex technical challenges. Our programs blend theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical experience to prepare students for successful careers in various engineering fields.

To be at the forefront of engineering education and research, producing skilled engineers who drive technological advancement and sustainable development.
