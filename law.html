Faculty of Law

The Faculty of Law at Nalanda University is committed to providing a comprehensive legal education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills. Our programs prepare students to become ethical legal professionals who can navigate complex legal challenges in a rapidly changing world.

Our Vision

To develop legal scholars and practitioners who uphold the principles of justice, equity, and the rule of law, and who contribute to legal reform and social progress.
