Nalanda

A Beacon of Learning

Nalanda University, located in Bihar, India, is a historic institution with a rich academic heritage that dates back to ancient times. Originally founded in the 5th century, it was one of the worldâ€™s first residential universities and became a renowned center of learning for scholars from across Asia and beyond. Offering studies in a wide range of subjects, including philosophy, science, and medicine, Nalanda attracted some of the greatest minds of the ancient world. After centuries of decline, the university was revived in the modern era with a commitment to academic excellence, interdisciplinary research, and global collaboration.
