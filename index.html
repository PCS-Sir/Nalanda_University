A Beacon of Learning

The University of Allahabad, established on September 23, 1887, is the fourth oldest university in India. Initiated by Sir William Muir, the then Lieutenant Governor of the United Provinces, it began as the Muir Central College and evolved into a degree-conferring institution. Over the years, the university has expanded its jurisdiction and affiliation, incorporating various colleges across the United Provinces.
